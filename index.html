Play Red Light Green Light with a friend! Find a long hallway or a large room. Place your phone, tablet, laptop, or other screen at the finish line.

Click on the triangle button (in the upper left corner of the screen) to view the settings. Adjust the difficulty settings and sound volume to suit the room size and age of the players. Click the triangle button again to hide the settings.

When you are ready, click the green light.  You will later click the red light to stop the game.

Run to the opposite side of the room to the starting position.

  • When the light is green, run toward the finish line!
  • When the light is yellow, slow down!
  • When the light is red, stop!

If you move when the red light is on, you have to go back to the starting line.

The first to make it to the finish line wins!
